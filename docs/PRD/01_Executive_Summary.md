# 📋 01 — Executive Summary

## Product Name
YourPilot

## Tagline
*"Life Changes. Your Plan Should Too."*

## Category
Adaptive Productivity Operating System

## One-Liner
YourPilot is an AI-powered productivity app that replans your day in real-time when life interrupts — so you stay on track without the guilt. Every task, every goal, every category is **100% defined by the user**.

## The Problem
Every productivity app fails when life happens. You plan 8 hours of work. Your friend shows up for 2 hours. Now your plan is broken, you feel guilty, you abandon the app.

## The Solution
StudyPilot AI's Dynamic Scheduler detects when you've lost time and instantly replans your remaining day — keeping your most important tasks, rescheduling flexible ones, and telling you exactly what to do next.

## Target Users
- Students preparing for exams or building skills
- Young professionals managing side projects alongside full-time work
- Anyone who wants to be productive but struggles with unpredictable days

## Key Differentiators
1. Real-time adaptive replanning (not static to-do lists)
2. Never guilt-driven — always recovery-focused
3. Priority-aware AI scheduling (user-assigned priorities, not AI-assumed)
4. "What should I do now?" — single-button clarity at any moment
5. XP rewards for consistency, not perfection
6. **Fully user-defined tasks** — no presets, no templates, no suggestions about what to work on

## Business Model (Future)
- Free tier: Core features
- Pro ($4.99/month): Advanced AI, analytics, voice, calendar sync
- Team ($9.99/user/month): Group productivity for teams

## Success Vision (1 Year)
10,000 daily active users who say YourPilot helped them stay consistent when life got messy — working on whatever matters to them.
